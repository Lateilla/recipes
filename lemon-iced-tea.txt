Ingredients:
- 4 green tea bags
- 4 orange pekoe tea bags
- 6 cups boiling water
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 (12 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- cold water, or as needed


Directions:
1. Place green tea bags and black tea bags in a 1-gallon glass jar. Pour boiling water over tea bags; steep for 30 minutes.

2. Remove tea bags; stir in sugar and lemonade concentrate until dissolved. Fill jar to the top with cold water; stir in lemon juice. Chill in the refrigerator until cold. Serve over ice.
